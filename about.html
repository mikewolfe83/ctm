About CTM

CTM is a nonprofit organization with a mission to promote post education in School and Apprenticeship to increase chances for success as an adult.

CTM is headquartered in Little Rock, AR. We are from Southwest Little Rock, and we are using our experiences growing up in the inner city and navigating life as adults.

Established in 2021 as a Motivational Content Organization, CTM wants to give our youth the insight and tips on how to navigate the corporate world . We want to develop and make content to expose our inner city youth to different career fields that they would not have the opportunity to see.

At the forefront of youth post education and apprenticeships, CTM wants to be the premiere after school and summer program to keep the youth engaged in investing time in their future success. We want to have a facility to bring inner city kids to reinforce and encourage post high school success and how to make a plan and strategy to execute.
